About Me

Me, somewhere with a nice view (2003)

Hi! I'm Katie, 24, originally from a very small town in Ohio that you have definitely never heard of. In 2002 I finished college, looked at my cubicle, and decided the cubicle could wait. I bought a one-way ticket and a 65-litre backpack and I haven't really stopped moving since.

I'm not a professional anything. I'm not sponsored. I pay for all of this by working seasonal jobs between trips — fruit picking, hostel reception, the occasional very questionable bar shift. I travel cheap, I sleep in dorms, and I have a deep and abiding love of overnight trains.

Why this website?

Because my mom kept asking where I was, and emailing the same story to 30 people got old fast. So I taught myself HTML out of a library book and built this page in Notepad. It is held together with hope and tables. Be kind.

My (very serious) packing philosophy

  • If you can't carry it up four flights of hostel stairs, leave it.
  • One good pair of socks beats three bad ones.
  • A roll of duct tape has saved every single trip at least once.
  • Always, always know how to say "thank you" in the local language.
